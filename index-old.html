How to use Giveter to buy gifts online in India.

To get to the the right short list of gifts you can provide information like age group (e.g. seniors, middle, youth, babies, children, etc), relationships (e.g. Husband, wife, boyfriend, girlfriend, father, mother, friend couple, etc), personality of the recipient (e.g sporty, creative, outgoing, traveler, geeky, book lover, etc), your mood (e.g. romantic, precious, personalized or funky). You can also chose gifts in a given price range. Availability of Cash on Delivery (COD) within India varies and can be known after you click on the buy button (on any product page) and you are taken to a specific partner online gift shop. Further you can also search gifts based on approximate days it takes to deliver to different cities in India. Flowers can be ordered online for more than 300 cities across India, same day delivery is possible for orders placed before 4 PM. There are other gifts that can be delivered on the same or next day in Delhi, Bombay (now Mumbai) or Bangalore; please get in touch to inquire about the same.

Social platform gifting in India

Giveter.com also provides a smart way to find gifts with strong integration with platforms like Facebook. Nothing will be posted on your or recipient's wall. So for example if your friend likes a partcular brand on Facebook, Giveter may suggest watches, accessories, clothes of this or similar brand.
